Plants of Texas

Allium canadense var. candense L. Alliaceae

Texas: Harris County. Baytown, lot just N of Roseland Park. Under
post-oaks on disturbed meadow. In wet brown clay. Perennial herb 20
cm tall, in tufts and masses- upright from bulbs. Bulbils present and
bulbs have garlic odor. Flower pinkish white corollas.

A. Traverse 2749 24 Jun. 1962
