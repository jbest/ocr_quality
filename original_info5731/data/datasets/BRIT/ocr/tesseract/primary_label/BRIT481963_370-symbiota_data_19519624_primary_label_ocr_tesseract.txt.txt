RED RIVER COUNTY:: 33.45 N, 95.05 W Lennox Woods
Nature Preserve, 10 mi N of Clarksville on SH 37, 1.5 mi W
along FM 2118 (to Mt. Pleasant community), and | mi S along
county road. Sloping upland S of Pecan Bayou. Sandy loam
(Kullit-Addielou and Woodtell complexes). Pine-hardwood
forest dominated by Pinus echinata, P. taeda, Quercus

falcata, Q. alba, and Liquidambar. Loosely tufted,
rhizomatous colonies. Scattered to common.
