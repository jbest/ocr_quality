TIERBARIUM OF SOUTHERN METHODIST UNIVERSITY

 

Varilla texana A. Gray

Zavala Co.: 7 miles n. of Crystal City.

Abundant in damp silty clay. Stems much
branched, decumbent at base, matted, forming low
broad clumps. Leaves very fleshy. Corollas greenish
yellow.
