HERBARIUM OF SOUTHERN METHODIST UNIVERSITY
Ampelenus

Harrison County: Caddo Lake State
Park, W. side along old abandoned
trail near bayou, sand, deep pine
forest. Trailing vine over shrub
2 meters or more high. Flowers

in clusters, greenish yellow,
odorless, tubular, 5 petals.
