Plants of Texas

Sapindaceae
Sapindus saponaria var. drummondii

Montague Co., Bowie, South side of Lake Amon Carter,
Western Cross Timbers Post Oak/Blackjack woodlands,
woods near shoreline northwest of pond. Native Perennial.
Truce-Owens Complex Soil Series. 5 - 25% slopes.
Associated Flora: Fraxinus texensis, Quercus marilandica,
Quercus stellata, Symphoricarpos orbiculatus, Ulmus
crassifolia, Juniperus virginiana.
