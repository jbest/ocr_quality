PLANTS OF TEXAS
HERBARIUM OF SOUTHERN METHODIST UNIVERSITY
Sisyrinchium pruinosum Bicknell
Anderson Co.: Long Lake (east side of Trinity River
bottoms), 12.6 miles southwest of Palestine.
Road fill, silty to fine sandy clay. Plant more or
less glaucous. Perianth blue-purple (pale outside)
with greenish yellow eye.
LLOYD H. SHINNERS 29,479
8 April 1962
