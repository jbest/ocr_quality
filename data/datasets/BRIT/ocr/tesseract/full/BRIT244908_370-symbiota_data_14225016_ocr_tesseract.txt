FLORA OF TEXAS

CYPERACEAE U.S.A., Texas
Chambers County

Cyperus cephalanthus Torr. & Hook.

Small, low-lying remnant clayey prairie on private
property along State Hwy. 124, between High Island and
Winnie. Locally frequent in small wetland with Paspalum
plicatulum, P. urvillei, Rhynchospora caduca,
Andropogon virginicus, Euthamia sp., Lythrum sp.,
Cyperus virens, C. pseudovegetus, Myrica sp., Sapium
sebiferum, Baccharis halimifolia, and Spartina patens.
