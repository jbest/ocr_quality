Cyperus

SAN PATRICIO CO.: rare on preserve, noted only
along fenceline between heavily grazed pasture on clay
soils and ungrazed grassy railroad right-of-way on
somewhat sandier soils, S side of tracks just W of corral
at N end of McGregor Ranch / Nueces Delta Preserve,
ca. 3.0-3.1 airmiles SSW of jct. US 77 and F. M. 631 at
Odem. Odem Quadrangle. N27°55.097', W097°36.776'.
Eley. 15 ft

Associates include Ambrosia psilostachya, Croton capitatus, Cynodon
dactylon, Gutierrezia texana, Helianthus praecox subsp. runyonii,
Lantana camara, L. urticoides, Malvaviscus arboreus, Panicum
maximum, Salvia coccinea, Setaria sp.
