Chambers County: Trinity River delta, ca. 1/4 mi. N of
mouth of small "gut". Altitude, 1 foot.

Marsh. Highly organic black clay in ca. 30 cm. water.
Spartina pectinata dominant. Also polygonaceous,
cyperaceous species.

Perennial herb, .7-1.0 m. In clumps, clonal. Flower:
white petals, yellow anthers.
