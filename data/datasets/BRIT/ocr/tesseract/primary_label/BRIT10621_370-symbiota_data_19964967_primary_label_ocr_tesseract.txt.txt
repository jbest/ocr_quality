Woodsia obtusa ssp. occidentalis

Montague Co., Bowie, South side of Lake Amon Carter,
Western Cross Timbers Post Oak/Blackjack woodlands, east
side of road in Post Oak woods on sandstone conglomerate
outcrop with mosses and liverworts. Native Perennial. Bonti-
Exray Complex Soil Series. 5 - 25% slopes. Associated
Flora: Galium aparine, Quercus stellata, Coreopsis tinctoria,
Solidago sp., Nuttallanthus texanus.
